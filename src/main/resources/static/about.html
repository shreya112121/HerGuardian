About HerGuardian

Empowering women to walk fearlessly at night with technology as their silent guardian.

🎯 Our Mission

HerGuardian was built with one goal: every woman should feel safe walking alone at night & anytime.

We combine real-time GPS tracking, intelligent check-in systems, and instant emergency response into one seamless digital safety companion.

No app should replace personal safety awareness, but we can be the safety net that's always there when you need it most.

⚙️ How It Works

  • Register and add your trusted emergency contacts
  • Start a walk session with your destination and expected arrival time
  • Our system tracks your live GPS location throughout the journey
  • Regular check-in prompts verify you're safe every 10 minutes
  • One tap SOS button alerts all contacts with your exact location
  • Emergency contacts and nearby police receive your GPS coordinates instantly
  • Mark yourself safe when you arrive — or help arrives fast

Your safety and privacy are our top priority:
